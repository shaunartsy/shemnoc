### About Page Copy

**H1:** `About SHEMNOC`

**Company Overview:**
SHEMNOC is a construction project management company that provides end-to-end solutions for clients who wish to bring their building and infrastructure projects to life — efficiently, effectively, and within budget.

Whether you are embarking on a residential, commercial, or civil construction project, SHEMNOC offers a complete suite of project management and consulting services, ensuring your project is delivered to the highest standards of quality, cost-efficiency, and time management.

Our team consists of seasoned Project Managers, Quantity Surveyors, and Construction Experts with vast industry experience in planning, construction monitoring, and closeout of projects of various sizes and complexities.

At SHEMNOC, we understand that managing a construction project can be stressful and time-consuming. That's why we offer our clients a single point of contact — so you can focus on your life or business while we handle all the details, communication, and coordination with contractors, suppliers, and consultants.

**Mission:**
To provide reliable, professional, and transparent project management services that transform our clients' ideas into high-quality, cost-effective, and timely completed projects.

**Vision:**
To become one of South Africa's most trusted and preferred project management companies — recognized for delivering excellence, maintaining quality, and achieving client satisfaction in every project we undertake.

**Our Software & Tools:**
MS Projects | Build Smart | CCS Candy | Primavera | ZOHO | Smartsheet

### Services Page Copy

**H1:** `Our Services`

**Phase 1 — Project Planning & Consulting**
- Face-to-face consultations on project initiation and execution
- Project cost estimates and BOQs
- Wayleave applications and approvals
- Budget estimates and cost planning
- Request for Quotations (RFQs) to sub-contractors (for client approval)
- Supplier comparisons and recommendations
- Final Bill of Materials (BOM) customized for your project
- Detailed project plan with timelines, resources, and cost breakdowns
- Comprehensive final report — ready for project execution

**Phase 2 — Project Implementation**
- NHBRC registration
- Council registration and compliance
- Wayleave applications and rezoning (where applicable)
- On-site supervision of suppliers, contractors, and subcontractors
- Procurement and sourcing of materials and equipment
- Quality assurance and timeline control at every stage
- Communication management — one point of contact
- Regular reporting, site visits, and progress updates

**Phase 3 — Project Closeout**
- Handover of completed works
- Final sign-offs from engineers, boards, and council
- Comprehensive project documentation and filing
